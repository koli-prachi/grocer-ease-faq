Registration

How do I register?

You can register by clicking on the "Register" link at the top right corner of the homepage. Please provide the information in the form that appears. You can review the terms and conditions, provide your payment mode details and submit the registration information.

Are there any charges for registration?

No. Registration on GrocerEase.com is absolutely free.

Do I have to necessarily register to shop on GrocerEase?

You can surf and add products to the cart without registration but only registered shoppers will be able to checkout and place orders. Registered members have to be logged in at the time of checking out the cart, they will be prompted to do so if they are not logged in.

Can I have multiple registrations?

Each email address and contact phone number can only be associated with one GrocerEase account.

Can I add more than one delivery address in an account?

Yes, you can add multiple delivery addresses in your GrocerEase account. However, remember that all items placed in a single order can only be delivered to one address. If you want different products delivered to different address you need to place them as separate orders.

Can I have multiple accounts with same mobile number and email id?

Each email address and phone number can be associated with one GrocerEase account only.

Can I have multiple accounts for members in my family with different mobile number and email address but same or common delivery address?

Yes, we do understand the importance of time and the toil involved in shopping groceries. Up to three members in a family can have the same address provided the email address and phone number associated with the accounts are unique.

Can I have different city addresses under one account and still place orders for multiple cities?

Yes, you can place orders for multiple cities.

My Account is the section you reach after you log in at GrocerEase. My Account allows you to track your active orders, credit note details as well as see your order history and update your contact details.

You need to enter your email address on the Login page and click on forgot password. An email with a reset password will be sent to your email address. With this, you can change your password. In case of any further issues please contact our customer support team.

Credit notes reflect the amount of money which you have pending in your GrocerEase account to use against future purchases. This is calculated by deducting your total order value minus undelivered value. You can see this in "My Account" under credit note details.

Pharma

What is pharma?

GrocerEase and TATA 1 MG have partnered to provide you the experience of TATA 1 MG within the GrocerEase app. Once you click on the pharma icon on the bb app, you will be redirected to the TATA 1 MG mobile website from where you can order medicines and book lab tests.

Delivery slots and timelines for pharma are different from those on GrocerEase?

GrocerEase only redirects you to the TATA 1 MG mobile website. The delivery timelines for pharma will be the same as defined on the TATA 1 MG mobile website for your location.

Prices of the same product are different on bb and bbnow compared to pharma?

The pricing of products on pharma is defined by TATA 1 MG and not GrocerEase. There will be variations in product pricing if you wish to buy the same product available on GrocerEase through TATA 1 MG.

What is the cancellation policy?

The cancellation policy for pharma is defined by TATA 1 MG. You can cancel the order until TATA 1 MG ships it. Orders once shipped cannot be canceled. GrocerEase cancellation policies will not apply to pharma.

What is the refund policy?

Refund policy is defined by TATA 1 MG. Please get in touch with the TATA 1 MG customer service team to log a refund request.

What is the return policy?

Return policy on pharma is defined by TATA 1 MG. TATA 1 MG accepts returns if the medicine provided to you does not match your order, or if the medicine is past its expiry date. Please check your orders at the time of delivery and report any return request before you pay our delivery personnel.

Are GrocerEase coupons and offers applicable on pharma?

No, GrocerEase coupons and product promotions are not applicable on pharma.

Will my pharma order be delivered along with my GrocerEase order?

No , pharma orders will be delivered by TATA 1 MG executives directly to your location. Delivery timelines will be defined by TATA 1 MG.
